(MIT ADT University Branch)

1️⃣ Why ACM on the Home Page (Main Focus)

🎯 Why highlight ACM directly?
	•	ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) is a globally recognized professional body.
	•	It adds credibility and prestige to the institution.
	•	Shows students are connected to international computing standards.
	•	Attracts new students, sponsors, and collaborations.

🏷 Branding Strategy
	•	Use official ACM logo.
	•	Use ACM’s blue & white color theme (same as official site).
	•	Provide a direct hyperlink to the official ACM website.
	•	Maintain consistent typography and design aesthetics similar to ACM.org.
